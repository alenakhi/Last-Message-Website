Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about The Last Message

What is The Last Message about?▼

The Last Message is an interactive experience Game that tells the story of Prophet Muhammad (peace be upon him) with historical accuracy and deep respect. It combines education with engaging gameplay to create an immersive journey through one of history's most significant narratives.

Is this game historically accurate?▼

Yes, we've consulted with Islamic scholars and historians to ensure authenticity and respectful representation of events. Every detail has been carefully researched and verified to provide an accurate portrayal of the historical narrative.

What platforms will it be available on?▼

The Last Message will be available on PC. We're also working on a mobile version to make the experience accessible to as many people as possible.

How can I support the development?▼

Join our community on Discord, subscribe to our YouTube channel for updates, and spread the word! Your support and feedback help us create the best experience possible.

When will the game be released?▼

We're currently in active development and will announce the release date soon. Follow us on our social channels and join our Discord for the latest updates and exclusive previews.
